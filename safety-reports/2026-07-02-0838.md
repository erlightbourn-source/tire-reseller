## (b) Flagged for review

1. **[Low — NEW] Reactivation restore threshold disagrees with auto-hide rule** — `app/api/auth/login/route.js:62` vs `app/api/reports/route.js:7`
   On soft-delete reactivation, listings are restored only if raw `_count.reports < 3`, but report auto-hide triggers at `AUTO_HIDE_AT = 5` **credible** (>24h-old account) reporters. A listing hidden purely by the soft-delete that has 3–4 raw reports (or ≥3 raw but <5 credible) stays incorrectly hidden after the owner reactivates. Fails conservative (over-hides, never under-hides), so low impact.
   - **Recommended fix:** recompute the credible-reporter count on reactivation and compare to `AUTO_HIDE_AT`, or export a shared `isAutoHidden(listingId)` helper from the reports module so the two code paths can't drift.
   - **Why human:** touches auth/account-flow behavior (reactivation) and moderation policy; the exact restore rule is a product decision.
2. **[Low — carried] Plaintext `email-alerts` confirm token** — benign opt-in action; unchanged, not worsened. Hash-at-rest like reset/verify tokens if hardening is ever desired.
